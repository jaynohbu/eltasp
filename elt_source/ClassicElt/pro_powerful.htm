  Powerful Business Tools  
     
 

ELT Pro empowers you to become an effective logistics provider with its powerful business tools because the right tools will make your business better prepare to keep up with rapidly changing demands in today’s competitive global market.

Access anytime and anywhere with the Internet 24x7
  Only a Web-based application gives you the freedom to work anytime and anywhere with a browser. You cannot do this with the conventional LAN based windows application. But since ELT Pro is a Web-based technology, it gives you full control of its application without such limitations.  
   
Improve application performance with ASP.NET
  ELT Pro is built on ASP.NET therefore the speed can no longer be an issue. ASP.NET technology makes application run faster because the commonly reused web pages are cached for a set period of time to improve the performance of web application. ASP.NET also allows web applications to interface with a database and the result is a powerful database-driven application. The last but not least is the security and reliability. Microsoft ASP.NET offers the most secure and reliable platform on the market today. .
   
Email, download and upload files
  In ELT Pro, you can upload and attach files such as invoice and packing list when emailing pre-alert and shipping notice to agents and clients. Likewise, you can also download documents such as airway bill, manifest and invoice and email them from your workstation.  
   
AES interface links directly with AES
  Using ELT Pro AES, you can submit SED information through an AES Web link to the US AES database. This direct link provides with a quick and easy access to SED over the Internet and eliminates re-entering the shipment data to make export operation a little simpler. 
   
EDI interface for AMS (coming soon)
  Interface with Import 2000, ELT Pro offers you an easy and single solution for both Air and Ocean import shipment. It is fully integrated, reduces data entry and sends AMS manifests to US Customs via EDI from a secure environment. 
   
Multiple shipment to a single consolidation with a click of mouse
  Multiple airway bills or bill of ladings can be assigned to a single consolidation with a click of mouse. You can see a list of consolidated cargo in master airway bill or master bill of lading screen and from there, you can work you way down to an individual airway bill or bill of lading. This makes consolidation task so much simple and easy.  
   
Intuitive and powerful search engine
  ELT Pro comes with a quick and powerful search engine. ASP.NET and Microsoft SQL 2000 based search engine will find all relevant information and allow you to jump directly to the specific data you want with hyperlink to that page. 
   
On-demand report with option to export to Excel and XML
  ELT Pro offers you with tools that you need to customize report according to your business requirement. The columns and fields can be hide, sorted and drag and drop to view only the information relevant to your need. Also the pre-defined search criteria allow you to get a quick search. Finally, you can also export it to XML and Microsoft Excel to further enhance your report.  
   
Comprehensive customer profile with option to export to Excel and XML
  The customer profiles are important feature in ELT Pro. Each data fields are carefully chosen to list requirements for all involved parties such as shippers, consignees, trucking, warehouses, CFS, terminal, government agents and agents. The rates, schedule B, bond info, website link, pop up email, comments and etc are also integrated to make it easy for you to see everything in one screen. Like in report, users can export company profile into Excel and XML so further enhance the data. 
   
Complete accounting systems with reporting tools
  ELT Pro offers a complete accounting package. A/R, A/P and credits/debits note (statement with agents) are automatically generated based on the task you create such as airway bill. You can easily clear and receive payment, write and print checks and enter journal entry and etc. The standard financial statements are also available. The reporting tools in A/P and A/R Aging Summary allow you to download into Microsoft Excel and XML to further enhance your data.
