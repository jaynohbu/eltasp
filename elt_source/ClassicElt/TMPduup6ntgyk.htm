  Company Overview  
     
 

Our goal is to provide high quality performance technology in the logistics industry and to deliver a program that will help our customer to be competitive in today’s global market.  

Company Profile 
e-Logistics Technology was founded in 2002 to provide an integrated solution for third party logistics providers. For a long time, freight forwarding community has been longing for technology that would allow industry professional to be more efficient and productive. Logistics is about exchanging goods in timely manner; reliability and accessibility therefore are the key factors in shipping business.  

e-Logistics Technology knows shipping business. e-Logistics Technology, not only comprise of a team of industry experts in forwarding, customs brokerage, warehousing and trucking, but it is the same members who have backgrounds in programming, supply chain, networking and database that makes them to better understand the shipping industry. e-Logistics Technology believes that technology must be able to handle areas of complexities involved with its process and to meet the need of traders partners, carriers and intermediaries involved in exchanging goods.
