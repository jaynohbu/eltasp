The Beta Client/Partner Profile is now released.
Thank you for your interest in Beta Client/Partner Profile. After we reviewed bugs and suggestions, we have implemented it to improve the overall user experience.

Client/Partner Profile - This area is where all contact and business information is kept for all the companies you do business with. This information will be used throughout the system, depending on what business type you assign to each record.

Rate Manager - This area is where you input your rate tables for Air Export. Air Import, IATA, Airline Buying, Customer Selling and Agent Buying rates can all be inputted. These rates will be automatically populated while making a shipment in the appropriate circumstances.

Port, Freight Location, Schedule B - This area is where you configure which of these types of information will show up in your system. This helps keep menus throughout the system at a manageable size, and makes sure only the items you need will be in those menus.

MAWB No. - This area is where you enter new blocks of MAWB numbers for your air carriers. You can also close out MAWB’s that are old so they remain in the system, but do not clutter up your menus.
