  Low Total Cost of Ownership  
     
 

ELT Pro is ASP application hosted in a secured environment. It comes with free software and hardware maintenance. This works perfectly for companies with small I.T budgets.

Minimal monthly fee
  Many software vendors charge initial setup fee with monthly per transaction or user-based fee. In addition, some software vendors charge you for upgrade and technical support. However, not everyone can afford to buy software like that especially if you have a tight budget. To accommodate these value-conscious customers, ELT Pro’s reasonable price will give customer more of technology’s benefit – profit. 
   
Hosted by one to one datacenter
  People at ELT understand sensitive nature of maintaining data for customer. Currently, ELT Pro servers are hosted by One to One Datacenter 24 x 7. Partnership with One to One, which runs one of that largest datacenters in the nation, you can be sure that your data is in good hands of I.T professionals at ELT and One to One. You also have an option to keep the data at your site if you want to do so. The technical consultants at ELT will work with you to find the best solution to meet your business requirements. .
   
Maintain real-time back up
  You don’t have to worry about backing up your data even if you don’t have I.T professionals or backup devices on site. People at ELT Pro manage and maintain data with the most advanced systems with redundant hardware because they have been there in Friday evenings when you can loss all your information and you only have an hour to meet an airline deadline. The technical consultants at ELT understand the importance of quality of service and having application back up for disaster recovery.   
   
No software installation required
  ELT Pro requires no software installation. The software installations are costly, time consuming and requiring skillful I.T person. The installed software also needs on-going maintenance, upgrade and etc. Sometimes installed software can cause conflict to create problems and this makes your business venerable and weak. With ELT Pro, you are free from all these potential risks because all you are doing is accessing it through Internet browser.  
   
No hardware installation required
  With Windows based software, you may have to purchase expensive servers and workstations. Because often time Window based software requires certain type of CPU and memory to run properly. If you can’t afford to buy new system, you can try installing on your current system. However, this will take up hardware resources from your current system therefore it will make your system to run slow. With ELT Pro, you don’t have to buy a new system. Most times it will work on your existing machine and this can be a huge cost saving for your company.  
   
Friendly technical supports with years of industry experience
  People who created ELT Pro are the same individuals who will help troubleshoot your problem. They have years of experience in shipping business so they understand your problem. You can be sure that people at ELT will give you the best support and help that you need.
